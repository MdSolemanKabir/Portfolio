About me

A director who lives in the details

I'm a New York–based creative director with tow years helping ambitious brands define their visual voice. I believe that the best design isn't decorative — it's strategic.

Before going independent, I led visual identity at two agencies, working across consumer goods, technology, and culture. These days I take on a small number of projects each year so every client gets my full attention.

When I'm not at the desk you'll find me at the darkroom, a bookstore, or on a long run through Prospect Park.
